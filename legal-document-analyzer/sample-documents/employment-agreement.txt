EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the 10th day of February, 2024

BETWEEN

TechCore Solutions Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 156, Tech Park, Bangalore 560001, represented by Mr. Vikram Singh, Vice President - Human Resources
(Hereinafter referred to as the "COMPANY" or "EMPLOYER")

AND

Ms. Priya Sharma, aged 28 years, residing at 25, Software Gardens, Bangalore 560076, Passport No. K1234567
(Hereinafter referred to as the "EMPLOYEE")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Company desires to employ the Employee in a professional capacity upon the terms and conditions mentioned hereinafter;

AND WHEREAS, the Employee desires to work for the Company in the said capacity;

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein and for other good and valuable considerations, the parties hereto agree as follows:

1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 The Employee shall be employed as Senior Software Engineer in the Product Development Division of the Company.

1.2 The Employee shall be responsible for developing and maintaining software applications as per the Company's requirements and shall report to the Development Manager.

1.3 The Employee's duties shall include but not limited to:
    a) Code development and maintenance
    b) Participating in design and code reviews
    c) Testing and debugging applications
    d) Documentation of technical specifications
    e) Collaboration with other team members

1.4 The Employee shall perform such other duties as may be reasonably assigned by the Company from time to time.

2. COMMENCEMENT DATE

2.1 The employment shall commence from the 1st day of March, 2024, unless extended or sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

2.2 The first 3 months from the commencement date shall constitute a probation period, during which either party may terminate the employment with 14 days written notice.

3. REMUNERATION AND BENEFITS

3.1 The Employee shall be paid an annual gross salary of Rs. 12,00,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs only) payable in monthly installments of Rs. 1,00,000/-, subject to necessary deductions for taxes and statutory contributions.

3.2 The salary structure is as follows:
    a) Basic Salary: Rs. 50,000/-
    b) Dearness Allowance: Rs. 20,000/-
    c) House Rent Allowance: Rs. 15,000/-
    d) Special Allowance: Rs. 15,000/-

3.3 The Company shall provide the following additional benefits:
    a) Medical Insurance: Comprehensive health coverage for the Employee and dependent family members
    b) Life Insurance: Group life insurance as per Company policy
    c) Provident Fund: Contribution as per statutory requirements
    d) Personal Accident Insurance: Coverage of Rs. 25,00,000/-

3.4 The Employee shall be entitled to the following leaves per calendar year:
    a) Casual Leave: 10 days
    b) Sick Leave: 12 days
    c) Earned Leave: 20 days
    d) Public Holidays as declared by the Government of India

3.5 Annual increment shall be considered after successful completion of probation period based on performance appraisal.

4. WORKING HOURS AND LOCATION

4.1 The Employee shall work 40 hours per week from Monday to Friday, with the office located at Tech Park, Bangalore. Remote work may be allowed as per Company policy.

4.2 The Employee may be required to work additional hours or on weekends if required by business needs, and shall be compensated as per Company policy.

4.3 The Company reserves the right to change the working hours, location, or nature of duties with reasonable notice.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 The Employee acknowledges that during employment, they shall have access to confidential information including source code, algorithms, business strategies, and client information.

5.2 The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all such information both during and after employment and shall not disclose the same to any third party without prior written consent of the Company.

5.3 All intellectual property, including source code, designs, inventions, and discoveries made by the Employee during the course of employment shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company.

5.4 The Employee shall execute all documents necessary to assign such intellectual property rights to the Company.

5.5 This confidentiality obligation shall survive termination of employment for a period of 2 years.

6. CONDUCT AND DISCIPLINE

6.1 The Employee shall maintain high standards of conduct and professionalism at all times.

6.2 The Employee shall comply with all Company policies, codes of conduct, and statutory laws.

6.3 Misconduct including but not limited to theft, dishonesty, insubordination, or violation of Company policies may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

6.4 The Company shall follow disciplinary procedures as per labor laws before any termination action.

7. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 During the tenure of employment and for a period of 2 years after termination, the Employee shall not engage in any business competitive to the Company's business within a radius of 500 kilometers.

7.2 The Employee shall not solicit or recruit any other employee of the Company for a period of 2 years after termination.

7.3 The above restrictions shall not apply if the termination is initiated by the Company without valid cause.

8. PERFORMANCE AND APPRAISAL

8.1 The Employee's performance shall be evaluated annually through a structured appraisal process.

8.2 Performance shall be measured on technical competency, project delivery, collaboration, and adherence to Company values.

8.3 Poor performance may result in counseling, performance improvement plan, or termination as per Company policy.

9. TERMINATION

9.1 Probation Period: Either party may terminate the employment with 14 days written notice.

9.2 After Probation:
    a) The Company may terminate for cause with 14 days written notice
    b) The Company may terminate without cause with 30 days notice and payment of severance equal to 3 months salary
    c) The Employee may resign with 60 days written notice

9.3 Cause for immediate termination without severance includes:
    a) Breach of confidentiality or non-compete clause
    b) Gross misconduct
    c) Violation of statutory laws
    d) Repeated violations of Company policies

9.4 Upon termination, the Employee shall:
    a) Return all Company property including documents, equipment, and access credentials
    b) Settle all outstanding dues
    c) Continue to be bound by confidentiality and non-compete provisions

10. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

10.1 The Company shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of termination of employment.

10.2 The Employee shall indemnify the Company from any claims, losses, or damages arising out of the Employee's breach of this Agreement or violation of any law.

10.3 The Employee's liability is limited to the salary payable for the notice period or actual loss, whichever is lower.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

11.1 Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be governed by the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, and applicable labor laws.

11.2 The parties shall attempt to resolve disputes through discussion with the Employee's manager and HR department.

11.3 If no resolution is reached within 30 days, the matter shall be referred to arbitration as per the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11.4 The place of arbitration shall be Bangalore.

12. GOVERNING LAW

12.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India, including the Indian Contract Act, 1872, the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), and the Code of Civil Procedure.

12.2 The courts of Bangalore shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes.

13. AMENDMENT AND MODIFICATION

13.1 No modification or amendment to this Agreement shall be valid unless made in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

13.2 If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in force.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

14.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

14.2 The Employee agrees to abide by Company policies and procedures which may be updated from time to time.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have hereunto executed this Agreement on the date first mentioned above.
