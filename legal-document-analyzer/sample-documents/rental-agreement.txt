RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Agreement of Lease is made and entered into on the 15th day of January, 2024

BETWEEN

Mr. Rajesh Kumar, aged 45 years, permanent resident of 42, Defence Colony, New Delhi 110024
(Hereinafter called the "LANDLORD")

AND

Mr. Amit Patel, aged 35 years, permanent resident of 15, Mumbai Street, Navi Mumbai 400706
(Hereinafter called the "TENANT")

WHEREAS, the Landlord is the rightful owner of the property situated at Apartment 3B, Sunrise Towers, 52 Lodhi Road, New Delhi 110003 (hereinafter called the "PREMISES"); AND

WHEREAS, the Landlord desires to lease the Premises to the Tenant, and the Tenant desires to lease the Premises from the Landlord, on the terms and conditions hereinafter mentioned;

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained and for other good and valuable considerations, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties hereto agree as follows:

1. PREMISES AND RENTAL PERIOD

1.1 The Landlord hereby lets to the Tenant and the Tenant hereby hires from the Landlord, the Premises described above for residential purposes only.

1.2 The Lease Term shall commence on the 1st day of February, 2024 and shall expire on the 31st day of January, 2025, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions hereof.

1.3 The Tenant shall take possession of the Premises on the commencement date with possession in clean and good condition.

2. RENT AND DEPOSITS

2.1 The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord a monthly rent of Rs. 45,000/- (Rupees Forty Five Thousand only) payable in advance on or before the 5th day of each calendar month.

2.2 The Tenant shall deposit with the Landlord a security deposit of Rs. 1,35,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Five Thousand only) at the time of signing this Agreement. This deposit shall be refundable at the end of the lease term, less any deductions for damages not caused by normal wear and tear or non-payment of rent or utilities.

2.3 The Tenant shall pay an additional amount of Rs. 2,000/- (Rupees Two Thousand only) per month towards maintenance and common area charges.

2.4 Late payment of rent shall attract a penalty of Rs. 500/- (Rupees Five Hundred only) per day payable by the Tenant.

3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE TENANT

3.1 The Tenant shall pay all utility charges including electricity, water, gas, internet, and telephone bills on time.

3.2 The Tenant shall not sublease the Premises or part thereof without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

3.3 The Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good and tenantable condition throughout the lease period and shall not commit any damage thereto.

3.4 The Tenant shall not keep any pets or animals in the Premises without the written permission of the Landlord.

3.5 The Tenant shall not use the Premises for any unlawful, immoral or commercial purpose whatsoever.

3.6 The Tenant shall permit the Landlord or his authorized representatives to inspect the Premises at reasonable hours and with prior notice of 24 hours.

3.7 The Tenant shall abide by all rules and regulations made by the society/association and shall cooperate with other residents.

4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE LANDLORD

4.1 The Landlord shall provide the Premises in a fit and habitable condition with all essential services connected.

4.2 The Landlord shall be responsible for structural repairs and maintenance of the building including roof, walls, and main plumbing.

4.3 The Landlord shall maintain the common areas and ensure all safety standards are met.

4.4 The Landlord shall not interfere with the quiet enjoyment of the Premises by the Tenant during the lease period.

5. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

5.1 The Landlord shall not be liable for any loss or damage to the Tenant's personal belongings, whether caused by theft, fire, water, or any other reason.

5.2 The Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless the Landlord from and against all claims, losses, damages, and expenses arising out of the Tenant's negligence, breach of this Agreement, or violation of any law.

5.3 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of this Agreement.

6. TERMINATION

6.1 This Agreement may be terminated by either party by giving 60 days written notice to the other party before the intended date of termination.

6.2 In case of breach of any material term of this Agreement by the Tenant, the Landlord may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

6.3 Upon expiry or termination, the Tenant shall vacate the Premises and return all keys to the Landlord in good condition.

6.4 If the Tenant continues to occupy the Premises after expiry without entering into a new agreement, he shall be liable to pay twice the monthly rent as penalty rent.

7. RENEWAL

7.1 Either party may indicate intention to renew this Lease at least 60 days before expiry.

7.2 The renewal shall be on mutually agreed terms and conditions which shall be incorporated in a new agreement.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall first be settled amicably through discussion between the parties.

8.2 If no amicable settlement is reached within 30 days, the matter shall be referred to arbitration by a single arbitrator to be mutually appointed.

8.3 The arbitration shall be conducted in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

8.4 The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding on both parties.

9. GOVERNING LAW

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the Indian Contract Act, 1872, the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), and the laws of India.

9.2 The courts of New Delhi shall have exclusive jurisdiction in respect of any dispute arising out of this Agreement.

10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

10.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations and understandings.

10.2 No modification or amendment of this Agreement shall be valid unless made in writing and signed by both parties.

10.3 If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue to be in force.

10.4 The waiver by either party of breach of any provision shall not constitute a waiver of any subsequent breach.
