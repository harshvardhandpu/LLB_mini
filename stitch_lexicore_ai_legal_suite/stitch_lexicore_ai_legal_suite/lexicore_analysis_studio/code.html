MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of October 15, 2023 ("Effective Date"), by and between Vanguard Technologies Inc. ("Client"), a Delaware corporation, and Nexus Solutions LLC ("Provider"), a California limited liability company.

1. SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES

Provider agrees to perform the services ("Services") and provide the deliverables ("Deliverables") set forth in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed by both parties. Each SOW shall be governed by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

2. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT

Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise specified in an SOW, Provider shall invoice Client monthly for Services performed.

!

Clause 2.2: Late Payments

"In the event Client fails to remit payment within fifteen (15) days of the invoice date, Client agrees to pay a late fee equal to five percent (5%) per month on the outstanding balance, compounded daily."

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Upon full payment of all fees due under an SOW, Provider grants Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Deliverables for Client's internal business purposes.

"Provider retains all right, title, and interest in and to any pre-existing materials, methodologies, or tools used in the creation of the Deliverables (the 'Background IP')."

4. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to maintain in strict confidence all non-public information disclosed by the other party ("Confidential Information"). This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.
